# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes telecom customer churn behavior using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The goal is to identify high-risk customer segments, understand churn drivers, analyze revenue risk, and provide business-focused retention strategies.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow including:

* Data Cleaning using Python
* SQL-based Business Analysis
* Interactive Power BI Dashboards
* Business Insights & Recommendations

---

## 🧩 Business Problem

Telecom companies face significant revenue loss when customers discontinue their services.

The major challenge is:

> How can the business reduce customer churn while improving long-term customer retention and revenue stability?

---

## ❓ Business Questions

1. Which customer groups are most likely to churn?
2. Which contract types contribute most to churn?
3. Does tech support reduce churn probability?
4. Which services create high-risk customer segments?
5. How much revenue is at risk due to churn?
6. What retention strategies can reduce churn?

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 1. Month-to-Month Customers Show Highest Churn

**Insight:** Customers with short-term contracts are more likely to discontinue services.

**Recommendation:** Encourage customers to switch to yearly contracts.

---

### 2. Customers Without Tech Support Have Higher Churn

**Insight:** Lack of technical support increases churn probability significantly.

**Recommendation:** Improve customer support adoption through bundled service offers.

---

### 3. Fiber Optic Users Contribute High Revenue Risk

**Insight:** Fiber optic customers generate high revenue but also show high churn rates.

**Recommendation:** Create retention campaigns specifically for fiber users.

---

### 4. Short-Tenure Customers Are High-Risk

**Insight:** Customers in early tenure periods churn more frequently.

**Recommendation:** Focus on improving first-year customer experience.
